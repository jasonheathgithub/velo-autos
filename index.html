FAQ

Common Questions

How does AI create ad creatives?

Our AI analyzes your inventory, brand guidelines, local market data, and competitor strategies to generate custom ad copy and visual concepts. Every creative is reviewed for compliance before launch.

Do I still need a human managing my ads?

VELO combines AI efficiency with human oversight. Our platform handles the heavy lifting — research, creative generation, optimization — while experienced strategists review campaigns and make high-level decisions. On our Overdrive plan, you get a dedicated strategist.

What about ad spend — is that included?

No, ad spend is separate from our management fees. You pay Meta (Facebook/Instagram) directly for your ad budget. We recommend a minimum monthly ad spend based on your market and goals, typically starting at $2,000–$5,000/month.

How long before I see results?

Most dealerships start seeing leads within the first 1–2 weeks. Meaningful performance data and optimization insights typically develop within 30 days. Full campaign maturity — where AI has enough data to consistently outperform — happens around 60–90 days.

Can you work with our existing CRM?

Yes. VELO integrates with most major automotive CRMs including DealerSocket, VinSolutions, elead, and more. Our Overdrive plan includes full CRM integration support to ensure leads flow directly into your existing workflow.

What if we already have an agency?

Many dealerships switch to VELO from traditional agencies for better performance at a lower cost. We offer a free competitive audit that shows how your current campaigns compare to what VELO can deliver. There's no pressure — the numbers speak for themselves.

Is there a contract or minimum commitment?

No long-term contracts. VELO operates on a month-to-month basis. We earn your business every month with results. We recommend at least 90 days to allow the AI to fully optimize, but you're free to cancel anytime.

How do you handle compliance?

Compliance is built into every step. Our AI is trained on FTC guidelines, Truth in Lending Act (TILA) requirements, and state-specific advertising regulations. Every ad creative is automatically checked before launch, and our team conducts regular compliance audits.
